Main Services Provided by the Organization
The Department provide analytical, consultancy and investigation services pertaining to forensic science and food science.These services are generally provided to public through the Police Department and the Courts of Law in legal matters or in cases of lawsuits. However some of the services are directly provided to the general public and to the private sector.
Services Offered:   Food Analysis   Analysis of Water   Examination of Questioned Documents   Vehicle Examination
