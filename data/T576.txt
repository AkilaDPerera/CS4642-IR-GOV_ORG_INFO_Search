Vision &amp; Mission of the Ministry
Vision : Providing assistance to sustain the services rendered to the public by the members of Parliament more efficiently.
Mission : Implementing policies plans and programmes productively in order to sustain the services rendered to the public by the members of Parliament more efficiently and contributing towards sustainability and improvement of culture.
Introduction of the Ministry
Year 2001 this Ministry has been established as a separate Ministry for the first time in the Parliamentary history of Sri Lanka.
We are dedicated to perform the duties of the Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs with responsibility and recognize the significance of its functions according to the national as well as internationally recognized policies.
