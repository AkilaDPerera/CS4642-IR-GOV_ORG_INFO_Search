Vision
To become the pioneer in the printing industry and the leader of exercise books and staioneries in the market.
Mission
To fulfill all the stationery needs of the public as an exeptional state corporation comprised with skillful and satisfied staff.
Background
The State printing corporation was set up in 1968 an Act of parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and now functions as a Statutory Institution under the purview of the Ministry of Mass Communication, and is a high quality printing house, equipped with pre-press and finishing operations. It is virtually capable of performing anything in the field of printing and publishing which also includes book binding.
Up to the year 1982 State Printing Corporation had primarily been engaged in printing of School books &amp; other Commercial printing work.
In addition to the above, in the year 1989 a publication project too was launched and it has established itself to become one of the leading printing works in the country.
The corporation comprises of two production units.
01.Main Press : Printing of the School Books and undertaken other Commercial printing work.
02. Stationery Factory: Production of Exercise Books and other Stationery Items are being processed in accordance with the
Current market requirements.
Service to the public
1.Printing of school text books
2.Manufacturing ofexercise books and other stationery items.
3.Security Printing Works(Such as lottery Tickets,Ridee Rekha Certificates,Post Offiece Lables,Dollar Tickets,Airport super Draw etc.)
*Printing of 'Mahajana Sampatha'Lottery tickets for National Lotteries Board has been carried out the last 30 years.
4.Special printing works(Printing ordres were obtained from Ministries,Government Departments,as well as private sector,Special printing works,including the following jobs too)
*Annual Reports of various institutions
*Various printing jobs of state pharmaseuticals Corporation
*University certificates
*National Zoological Garden-Tickets &amp; Leaflets
*Cinema Tickets
*Printing Jobs of Samurdhi Authority
*National Savings Bank-Printing jobs of National Savings Bank,including 'Ridee Rekha'certificates.
Sales &amp; Marketing
Production &amp; Distribution
*SPC Produce high quality exercise books,stationaery &amp; new productions from various papers according to customers requirements to their distribution.There are number of SPC outlets in island wide.
Advertising &amp; Promotion
*SPC has launched a sales promotional pragram through electronic &amp; print media during the peak season
*SPC has taken steps to implement natinal and provincial level activities for promotional purpose.Such as dayata kirula National Exibition,International book fair &amp; activities parallel for the world's children's day &amp; various exibitions arranged by schools &amp; institutions.
*We distribute SPC products free of charge for community development projects as propaganada.
