Introduction
Department of Manpower and Employment was established with effected from 1st January 2010 under the Ministry of Labour Relations and Productivity Promotion, in terms of the Gazette Extraordinary no 1640/30 on 12th February 2010.
This department is currently functioning under the purview of the Ministry of Productivity Promotions.
Vision
World class Sri Lankan work force.
Mission
Development of a globally competitive work force and use the full potential of our human resources for the socio economic development of Sri Lanka.
Values
Professionalism, team work and passion for progress, people oriented, strategy oriented, result oriented and competency oriented.
